The Log Cabin

The Log Cabin is the perfect spot if you are looking for a restaurant to take the family. Having been rebuilt in 2022 with a completely new bar, kitchen, floor and beer garden, The Log Cabin is perfect for lunch or dinner time.

The Log Cabin also includes two different areas upstairs. A function room where people of the public can hire for different events that they wish to hold, from corporate events, birthdays and even weddings. The other section is the Sinclairs fine dining restaurant where you will be treated like royalty from the lovely staff.

Revolver Lounge

The bar that they have serves anything from shots, to signature and classic cocktails to coffee and teas. Their signatures being original recipes and takes on classic cocktails, will be sure to leave you flabbergasted after the first sip and leave you wanting more.

Mr Watkins

Located in High Street Penrith, Mr Watkins is a cafe by day, and an old fashioned bar and restaurant by night. Their specialties lie in quality service, both from the kitchen and behind the bar. They have an astounding list of 70+ cocktails, both classics and signatures. Not to mention the quality beers, wines and extensive spirit list that they have.

They also host functions at the establishment. Anything from a birthday, all the way to a baby shower can be held here. As Mr Watkins prides themselves on flexibility when it comes to functions, they are confident that they can find a solution for every budget and occasion.
